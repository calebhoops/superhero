Who is He?

About

Frank Castle aka the punisher, first appeared in spiderman in 1979. Often seen as being obseesed with vengeance. The punisher can use very violent tactics to kill enemies.

Abilities

The Punisher is very well trained in military tactics and skills. He has gone through the US Marines, Force reconnaissance, and cross trained with the Australian Special Air Service. He has great ability in special operations and use of tactical weapons.

Accomplishments

The punisher has defeated many gang members while being outnumbered. He has also escaped major prisons. Few of his foes comeback to fight because of his homicidal nature
